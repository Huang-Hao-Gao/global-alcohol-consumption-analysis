# global-alcohol-consumption-analysis

The stakeholder is the Head of Market Expansion at an international alcohol producer, responsible for deciding which countries to prioritise for market entry, deeper investment, or deprioritisation. 
Their goal is to identify markets with strong and reliable demand
They are asking these questions to reduce the risk of entering volatile markets, focus resources on the most attractive opportunities, and support decisions around rollout sequencing, forecasting confidence, and long-term growth strategy
rollout sequencing - order in which countries are entered
forecasting confidence - how confident the company is in its sales predictions
long-term growth strategy - how the company plants to grow over many years
